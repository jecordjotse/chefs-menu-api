Vegetable Soup

Every sip of warm and healthy vegetable soup is a divine experience during cold winter days. This recipe uses a variety of vegetables and vegetable stock to make it more healthy and tasty. This simple yet flavorful soup recipe is not just easy to follow but also customizable to bring in your favorite taste with selected veggies. It calls for using fresh vegetables for best results; however, you can also use canned ones.
